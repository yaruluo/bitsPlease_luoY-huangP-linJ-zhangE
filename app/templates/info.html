Info


Hi, we're so glad you're here! This project makes use of census data compiled from 2019, sourced from Kaggle.com. You can find our dataset here.


How to navigate


To see a particular demographic throughout the entire US, click country and toggle the second dropdown menu for the data you want. The map displays a general chloropleth of the demographic density throughout the US. The chart displays a bubble chart for density size, radially-stacked bar chart for ethnicity build, or double bar chart for gender ratio by state.

On the map, click on a state or search for a state on the state tab to view particular info by state/county.


Types of info:

  • Population density throughout the country
  • Gender ratios throughout the country
  • Ethnicity dispersion throughout the country
  • Population of each county per state
  • Voting percentage of each state
  • Median income of each county per state


Types of data visualization charts:

  • Bubble charts
  • Chloropleth maps
  • Pie charts
  • Bar charts
  • Radially-stacked bar charts
